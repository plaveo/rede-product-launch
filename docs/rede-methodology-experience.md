# 01 — REDE Methodology Experience Blueprint

**Status:** Official foundation document. Defines the *experience* of the REDE method — not screens, not UI.
**Purpose:** Make the methodology itself the product. Every future screen, workflow, and feature derives from this document.

**REDE North Star:** REDE does not decide for anyone. It gives a real estate professional accurate, organized, evidence-based property intelligence so they can explain a property with greater confidence. The decision always belongs to the client. Belief chain: **facts → confidence → better communication → client understanding → better decisions.**

---

## THE LOCKED FLOW (never reorder)

```
PROPERTY
   ↓
30 SIGNALS
   ↓
5 INTERPRETATION LENSES
   ↓
1 DECISION
   ↓
PROFESSIONAL REPORT
```

The professional moves in one direction only. No stage can be skipped. Each stage must feel complete before the next unlocks. This linearity is the source of confidence — the professional always knows where they are in the process and why.

---

## STAGE 01 — PROPERTY

**Purpose:** Define what enters the REDE Engine.

**The experience:** Before any analysis begins, the professional establishes a single, unambiguous subject. REDE analyzes *one property at a time*. The professional is never confused about what is being evaluated.

**What is being analyzed:** One specific property — a unit, a house-and-lot, or a development — identified precisely enough that two professionals looking at the same input would agree they are analyzing the same thing.

**Information required before analysis begins (the Property Foundation):**
- **Identity** — project/building name, unit or lot reference, developer.
- **Location** — address and position precise enough to anchor later location signals.
- **Type & configuration** — property type, size, layout, floor/level where relevant.
- **Purpose of analysis** — own-use vs. investment (the founder's original 2-way decision). This single input reframes how later signals are weighed.
- **Price context** — asking price or reference price, so value signals have an anchor.

**How the professional understands the subject before processing:** REDE presents the Property Foundation back as a confirmed summary. The professional sees exactly what will be analyzed and confirms it. Nothing proceeds on assumption. If a required foundation item is missing, REDE names it plainly rather than guessing — this is the first act of building trust.

**Output of Stage 01:** A confirmed Property Foundation. The subject is locked. The professional feels: *"REDE knows exactly what we are analyzing."*

---

## STAGE 02 — 30 SIGNALS

**Purpose:** The complete observation stage. Gather every fact before any interpretation.

**The experience:** Observation and judgment are deliberately separated. In Stage 02 the professional only *collects and confirms facts* — no conclusions yet. This discipline is what makes REDE evidence-based instead of opinion-based.

**How the 30 Signals are organized:** The 30 Signals are grouped into **5 clusters of 6**. Each cluster is the raw material for one of the 5 Interpretation Lenses in Stage 03. Collecting in these clusters means observation and interpretation stay aligned — the professional gathers with structure, not randomly.

> Proposed signal architecture (founder to lock the exact 30):

**Cluster A → feeds Location & Environment lens**
1. Immediate neighborhood quality
2. Accessibility & transport links
3. Nearby demand drivers (offices, malls, schools, transit)
4. Surrounding development activity / supply pipeline
5. Environmental exposure (flood, noise, congestion)
6. Future infrastructure affecting the area

**Cluster B → feeds Property & Condition lens**
7. Physical condition / turnover state
8. Layout efficiency & usable space
9. Floor level / position / view
10. Building or development quality & amenities
11. Developer track record & reliability
12. Age and maintenance status

**Cluster C → feeds Value & Financial lens**
13. Asking price vs. comparable units
14. Price per sqm vs. area benchmark
15. BIR zonal value anchor
16. Transaction & holding costs (CGT, fees, dues)
17. Financing terms available
18. Total cost of ownership over the holding period

**Cluster D → feeds Market & Demand lens**
19. Absorption / how fast comparable units move
20. Vacancy level in the cluster
21. Rental demand & achievable yield
22. Occupancy performance of comparable rentals
23. Buyer/tenant profile for the property
24. Dependency risks in local demand (single-industry reliance)

**Cluster E → feeds Risk & Legal lens**
25. Title & ownership status
26. Documentary completeness
27. Developer/turnover or delivery risk
28. Association / dues / management stability
29. Regulatory or zoning constraints
30. Liquidity — how easily this can be exited later

**How Signals are completed:** Each Signal is confirmed with a value or a clear "not available." A Signal is never silently skipped. When data is missing, REDE marks it as an explicit gap rather than pretending — because an honest gap is more trustworthy than a fabricated fact.

**How progress is visualized:** The professional always sees how many of the 30 are confirmed and which cluster they are in (e.g., "18 of 30 confirmed — completing Value & Financial"). Progress is felt as momentum, not burden.

**How the professional knows all Signals are processed:** Stage 02 closes only when all 30 are either confirmed or explicitly marked as gaps. REDE then presents an Observation Summary: complete facts on one side, honest gaps on the other. The professional feels: *"Every fact is on the table — nothing hidden, nothing guessed."*

**Output of Stage 02:** A complete 30-Signal observation record, with confidence flagged by how many gaps exist.

---

## STAGE 03 — 5 INTERPRETATION LENSES

**Purpose:** Transform raw observations into intelligence.

**The experience:** The 30 confirmed facts are now read through 5 lenses. This is where scattered data becomes *meaning*. Each lens takes its 6 signals and produces one clear interpretation.

**How the 30 Signals become 5 Lenses:** Each cluster of 6 signals resolves into one lens verdict. The professional literally watches observation become intelligence — 30 facts folding into 5 readings.

**What each Lens represents:**
1. **Location & Environment** — Is the location working for or against this property?
2. **Property & Condition** — Is the property itself sound and well-built?
3. **Value & Financial** — Is the price fair, and does the math hold?
4. **Market & Demand** — Is there real, durable demand?
5. **Risk & Legal** — What could go wrong, and how exposed is the buyer?

**How the professional moves through each Lens:** One lens at a time, in order. Each lens shows: the 6 signals behind it, the interpretation REDE draws, and a confidence level for that reading. The professional can trace every interpretation back to the specific facts that produced it — no black box.

**How Lens interpretation is presented:** Each lens gives a plain-language reading (strength, neutral, or concern) plus the reasoning. Interpretation is always explainable — the professional must be able to repeat REDE's logic to a client in their own words. That repeatability is the confidence REDE is built to create.

**Output of Stage 03:** 5 Lens interpretations, each with reasoning and a confidence level. The professional feels: *"I understand this property from every angle, and I can defend each read."*

---

## STAGE 04 — 1 DECISION

**Purpose:** Transform intelligence into one professional decision.

**The experience:** The 5 lenses converge into a single, clear position. Not five opinions — one decision the professional can stand behind.

**How the final decision is produced:** REDE weighs the 5 lens readings against the purpose set in Stage 01 (own-use vs. investment). The same property can read differently for different purposes — REDE respects that. The result is one consolidated position.

**How GO / CAUTION / AVOID is generated:**
- **GO** — lenses align positively, risks are low or manageable.
- **CAUTION** — meaningful strengths but real concerns; proceed only with specific conditions addressed.
- **AVOID** — one or more lenses reveal problems serious enough to outweigh strengths.

**How confidence is displayed:** The decision carries an explicit confidence level, driven by how complete the 30 Signals were and how strongly the 5 lenses agree. Full data + aligned lenses = high confidence. Gaps or conflicting lenses = lower confidence, stated openly.

**How uncertainty is communicated:** REDE never hides doubt. If confidence is low, it says so and names exactly why (which gaps, which conflicting lenses). A CAUTION with clear reasons is more valuable to a professional than a false GO. This is the discipline that protects the professional's credibility with their client.

**The boundary (non-negotiable):** REDE produces a *professional decision recommendation* — an evidence-based position for the professional to present. It does not decide for the client. The final choice always belongs to the buyer. REDE's job is to make the professional's explanation confident and defensible.

**Output of Stage 04:** One decision — GO / CAUTION / AVOID — with a stated confidence level and the reasoning behind it.

---

## STAGE 05 — PROFESSIONAL REPORT

**Purpose:** Transform the decision into professional output the professional can hand to a client.

**The experience:** Everything the professional just processed becomes a clean, defensible document. The report is proof of thinking, not decoration. It lets the professional present with authority and lets the client understand *why*.

**What information belongs in the report:**
- The Property Foundation (what was analyzed and for what purpose).
- The decision (GO / CAUTION / AVOID) with its confidence level, stated up front.
- The 5 Lens interpretations, each with its reasoning.
- The 30 Signals as supporting evidence, including honest gaps.
- A plain-language summary the client can follow without expertise.

**How the methodology is documented:** The report mirrors the locked flow — Property → Signals → Lenses → Decision. The reader can follow the exact path REDE took, so the conclusion never feels arbitrary.

**How evidence is referenced:** Every interpretation in the report traces back to the specific signals behind it. Every claim is anchored to a fact or an acknowledged gap. Nothing is asserted without support.

**How the professional/client understands why REDE reached its conclusion:** By reading the report top to bottom, the logic is self-evident: these facts → these interpretations → this decision, at this confidence level. The report answers "why" before it is asked.

**Output of Stage 05:** A complete Professional Report — the tangible result of the REDE method, and the artifact the professional uses to explain the property with confidence.

---

## THE REPEATABLE PROCESS (what the professional internalizes)

After using REDE a few times, the method becomes second nature:

> **Confirm the property. Observe 30 facts. Read them through 5 lenses. Reach 1 decision. Produce the report.**

Same order, every time. That repeatability is the product. The professional stops guessing and starts running a disciplined, defensible process — which is exactly the confidence REDE exists to create.

## RULES HONORED IN THIS DOCUMENT

- The methodology is defined as a repeatable professional decision process.
